ABOUT SOKO

Soko is an online delivery service, and a market place for groceries that can be acquired from the market and shops at a relatively cheaper price than the real market price. Soko does fast, often same-day deliveries of any kind of a package that might be needed for delivery within a particular area around Kampala. It specializes in all kinds of deliveries such as fast foods, groceries bought from a market, home appliances, documents, envelopes and many others as long as they can be delivered by our vehicles, i.e. by bicycles, motorcycles, mini trucks and vans basing on the quantity of the package. The client is able to receive his /her package within a desired time and they are transported in safe hands.
